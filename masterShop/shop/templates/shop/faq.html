Users Questions

What if I need to return something?

No worries! Contact us right away and we'll help guide you through the process - we'll send you a prepaid mailer to send the item back.

From the time I place my order, how long will it be before I receive it?

When placing your order, please note the following: For all orders within the USA, please allow 5 - 14 business days for delivery. For all orders outside of the USA, please allow 10 - 21 business days for delivery.

If you have not received your order within the time frame listed above, then please allow at least 3 weeks prior to contacting us to check on the status of your shipping.

My order is missing parts or is broken. What do I need to do?

Please contact our customer service department at support@MasterShop.com and we'll help you resolve the problem!
